Hannah Maddy

Primary tenant

Hannah Maddy works as a product designer for Netflix. She has been working as an UX/product designer for 8+ years and has done work for brands such has Fuel1 Racing, The Ritz Carlton, Corona, Ford, Rockstar Games, Geico, Dell, AT&T, F5 Networks, and more. Hannah is also half Japanese and is fluent in Japanese.


Hannah owns a home in Seattle and is currently renting it out to tenants. She bought her home in 2016 at 7706 Delridge Way SW and has enjoyed redesigning and landscaping her home before the move to San Francisco.


On her free time, Hannah enjoys hiking, trips to dog parks, exploring new eateries, interior design, and whisky tastings.
