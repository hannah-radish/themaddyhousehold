Scobie Maddy

Primary pet tenant

Scobie is a 7.5 year old neutered Jack Russel Terrier/Pug mix. Hannah adopted Scobie over 3 years ago and he has been living in apartment rentals up to the point when Hannah purchased her home in 2016.


Scobie is 15 lbs and up-to-date with all of his shots. He is very social and playful and loves befriending other dogs, children and adults. Scobie is 100% potty trained and enjoys getting his energy out with a walk, a trip to the dog park or a good game of fetch.


Scobie has a huge selection of toys values and enjoys playing with them on his own - he even throws his own ball for fetch!
