The maddy's

Comet Maddy

Primary pet tenant

Comet is a 11 year old neutered Chihuahua. Hannah adopted Comet over 9 years ago and he has been living in apartment rentals up to the point when Hannah purchased her home in 2016.


Comet is 8 lbs and up-to-date with all of his shots - his groomer and vets love handling him due to his extremely calm and mellow temperament. Comet is extremely well-mannered, never barks and is 100% potty trained. With his old age, he has slowed down quite a bit and spends most of days sleeping and enjoys spending time in the sunshine.


In Hannah’s previous jobs, Comet has come to work with her and quietly spent his time by her side. He will come when called but very rarely leaves Hannah’s side.
